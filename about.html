About Saint Ignatious College

About Us

Saint Ignatious College was established in the year 2002 in Johannesburg. It was found by Mr. Ignatious Kangausaru as a Catering School. The College transformed and diversified into different courses. In 2003, we started offering short courses and full qualifications were also introduced. NOW THE COLLEGE BOASTS OF SIX BRANCHES NATIONWIDE.

Education is both enlightening and empowering, enabling you to contribute positively to the socio-economic development of your community and nation. One cannot negate the fact that today’s is an increasingly unstable one through the rapid changes in technology in the communication and related fields. These rapid changes, in turn, have a huge impact on the socio-cultural and economic environment. The individual has to be equipped for these changes if he/she is to fit into and play a meaningful role in society. Thus at SAINT IGNATIOUS COLLEGE, we are committed to providing all our students with the highest standard of education. This is through the creation of an enabling learning environment, provision of face to face and one-on-one tuition by experienced and committed tutors; learning can be on a part-time or full-time basis. We also provide excellent, user-friendly study guides for a wide range of our courses. You are expected to undertake coursework assignments based on these modules before you sit for a final examination. Through dedication and commitment to study and co-operation with tutors, you should be able to pull through excellently.”I wish you the best in your studies. CHOOSE SAINT IGNATIOUS COLLEGE…THERE IS NO BETTER ALTERNATIVE.” .
